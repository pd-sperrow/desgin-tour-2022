About this event

Design Tour Sreemangal, 2022

Just like every year we (Design Community Sylhet) organize a great design tour and workshop in Sreemangal, Bangladesh. This event will be a fantastic opportunity to learn more about design, meet and interact with experienced designers around the globe.

We have successfully completed three design meet-up's and many design workshops in the recent years.
